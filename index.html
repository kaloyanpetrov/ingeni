The Visionary

Who stays behind INGENI?

Vessela Jeleva, the creative force and founder of INGENI, brings over a decade of international experience in high-end interior architecture. Her approach is defined by a meticulous eye for detail and a deep understanding of spatial psychology.

"Design is not just about aesthetics; it's about how a space breathes and how it makes you feel." Vessela continues to push the boundaries of minimalist luxury, creating homes that are both functional masterpieces and soulful retreats.
